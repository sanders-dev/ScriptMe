Common Objections/ Rebuttals


Risk

  • I may never use it


Rebuttal/ reward

  • 84% use coverage in first 2 years

  • Why do they only give you a 3-4 year warranty

  • Service department makes 76% of dealers revenue

  • 10 times more likely to use warranty vs. car insurance

  • I hope you never use it; its all about the P.O.M

  • If you did need it, at least the repair would only cost $100/ $50


Risk

  • You could go out of business


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • In business for 30+ years

  • Backed by A+ rated insurance

  • Warranties sold through dealers, solid foundation


Risk

  • You’re not my dealer


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • Our warranties are sold through dealerships nationwide

  • We are on direct pay with the dealers (VISA, MC)

  • Nothing changes- give example / Paint picture

  • After you warranty is out, you’re no longer part of the dealer either

  • They do not pay your repair bill, we do –give example


Risk

  • I don’t know you


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • Unless you’ve worked in a service dept., we don’t expect you would

  • Pull up their dealership to create credibility- Give example / Paint picture

  • In business for 30+ years

  • Backed by A+ rated carrier

  • Sold through dealers & accepted at all dealers nationwide (VISA, MC)

  • Nothing changes


Risk

  • I don’t like giving financial information out over the phone


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • Safer than mailing (lost or stolen)

  • Fraud protection on CC transactions

  • We record our phone calls for quality assurance

  • This is why we start with a small DP to earn their trust


Risk

  • I feel pressured


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • Pressure is when you’re at the dealer and they want $1000 today for repairs

  • Pressure is being without a vehicle and having no way to get to work

  • We are here to help, to alleviate pressure- Give example


Risk

  • I don’t like making snap decisions


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • You’re not making a snap decision; you’ve had the vehicle for 2-3 years: You do plan on keeping it?

  • If you have a repair tomorrow, they don’t allow you to think about it

  • You’re not making a 3, 4, 5 year commitment, transfer and/or pro-refund


Risk

  • My spouse will get mad at me


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • Who drives the vehicle?

  • Who pays the bills?

  • Who will take it in?

  • Protecting your investment by buying a necessity, not a luxury

  • Will spouse be angrier for purchasing a warranty or when the transmission goes out; draining your savings?

  • Smarter decision to keep car than trade it in


Risk

  • I don’t know if I can afford it


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • How could you afford a $1500 repair?

  • Would you rather pay $1500, or $100 next time you go in for a repair?

  • Protects your budget

  • Allows you to keep car longer, which ultimately saves you money from having to purchase a new car until you are ready

  • Always have transportation (rental car) to get to work- Give examples


Risk

  • I may sell or trade it


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • That’s the best thing about our program. If you sell it, trade it, wreck it, or even just change your mind; transfer/ refund available –Give example

  • You won’t be forced to trade early

  • It will be easier to sell with a warranty, than “as is”

  • New cars only give you 3 years of coverage, this is for ______ years


Risk

  • I never buy something without seeing it in writing


Rebuttal/ Reward

  • What questions did you have? What are you unclear about? (Paint pictures)

  • Refer to owners manual

  • Our coverage is sold through dealers, offering the same program they do

  • We only require a small DP so we can mail everything priority mail

  • You sign up for car and health insurance without reading everything
